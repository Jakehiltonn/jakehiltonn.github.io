Recipe #1

Pasta From Trader Joe's

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 persons
Ingredients Quantity
Hard Shells 2 Cups
Cheese Sauce 1/2 Cup
Milk 2 Tablespoons

Step 1: Fill up pot with 8 cup of water and boil till it bubbles.
Step 2: Pour hard shells into water and stir. Cook for 8 minutes.
Step 3: Once shells are soft and ready to eat, pour shells and water into a strainer and slowly shake till
all water drains.
Step 4: Pour shels back into pot.
Step 5: Open packet of cheese sauce and pour over shells. Then add 2 tablespoons of milk and stir till
you are satified.
Step 6: ENJOY!!

Recipe #2

Cereal

  • Time: 2 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Cereal (of your choice) 1 Bowl
Milk 2 Cups
Bowl 1
Spoon 1

Step 1: Open the desired cereal and pour into the bowl (DO NOT POUR MILK FIRST)
Step 2: Pour milk over cereal slowly till cereal can almost overflow
Step 3: Put spoon in bowl and ENJOY!

Recipe #3

Quesadilla

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Tortilla 1
Cheese How ever much
you want
Guacamole 1 Packet
Butter 1/10 Stick

Step 1: Turn stove and put butter on. Once butter is melted, put tortilla on.
Step 2: Put cheese in tortilla and fold up. Let sit for 5 min.
Step 3: Flip constantly until a nice golden brown color starts to show
Step 4: Get out plate and place quesadilla on. Open Guacamole and ENJOY!

Recipe #4

Taquitos

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1-2 persons
Ingredients Quantity
Money $8.50
Oven 1
Taquitos 5-10
Tinfoil 1 12x12
inch sheet

Step 1: Go to local taquito selling store and use money to buy a pack of frozen taquitos.
Step 2: Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Step 3: Take out sheet of tinfoil and lay taquitos in a row on the sheet.
Step 4: Once oven is preheated, place taquitos in and let cook for 20 minutes.
Step 5: Let the taquitos cool for a couple minutes, then ENJOY!
