Chocolate Rice Crispy Treats

  • Time: 20-30 minutes
  • Serves: 5-15 persons
Ingredients Quantity
Vegetable shortening 2 tablespoons
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® cereal 3 cups
Miniature Marshmallows 2 cups
Chocolate Chips 2 Cups

Step 1: In 3-quart saucepan, melt chocolate and shortening over low heat, stirring
constantly until smooth. Remove from heat.
Step 2: Add KELLOGG'S RICE KRISPIES cereal and marshmallows to chocolate mixture.
Stir until well coated.
Step 3: Spread mixture evenly into 8 x 8 x 2-inch pan coated with cooking spray. Chill
until firm. Cut into squares. Store covered in refrigerator.
Step 4: ENJOY!!!
